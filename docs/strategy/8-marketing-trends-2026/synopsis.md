## Key Tactics Extracted

### 1. On-Platform Lead Capture (High Signal)

**Tactic:** Stop sending social traffic to external landing pages. Capture leads inside the platform using tools like ManyChat.

**Why:** Platforms throttle reach when you link out. Mobile users drop off on external pages.

**How:**
- Set up keyword triggers in comments
- Auto-DM users who engage
- Capture info without them leaving the platform

**Applicability:** Any project with social presence. B2C especially. Works for SaaS free trials, newsletters, communities.

**Priority:** HIGH — Low effort, measurable impact.

---

### 2. Search Everywhere Optimization (Medium Signal)

**Tactic:** Optimize content for discovery across Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, ChatGPT, LinkedIn — not just Google.

**How:**
- Weave keywords into scripts, captions, on-screen text, descriptions
- Build topic clusters (multiple posts around same theme)
- Optimize for conversational queries (full phrases, not keywords)

**Applicability:** Any content-driven project. Essential for B2C, valuable for B2B thought leadership.

**Priority:** MEDIUM — Requires content volume. Not a quick win.

---

### 3. AI Output Validation (High Signal)

**Tactic:** Don't trust AI output blindly. Hallucination rates are similar across free/paid tools.

**How:**
- Use AI for first drafts, not final drafts
- Cross-reference every stat/claim
- Build internal quality checks
- Use AI for ideation, not execution

**Applicability:** Universal. Any team using AI for content/strategy.

**Priority:** HIGH — Process change, not tool change. Free to implement.

---

### 4. Get Cited by ChatGPT (Medium Signal)

**Tactic:** Create listicles in micro-categories to train ChatGPT to cite you as authority.

**How:**
- Publish "top 10" lists in specific niches (not broad categories)
- Use digital PR to get other authoritative sites to cite you
- Format content for easy extraction: headers, bullets, tables
- Add schema markup and "last updated" dates
- Include case studies, stats, first-party data

**Applicability:** Any project with a website. Most valuable for B2B SaaS, agencies, consultants.

**Priority:** MEDIUM — Long-term play. Results compound but take time.

**Caveat:** Heavy self-promotion for his agency here. The core advice (digital PR + structured content) is sound, but the specific tactics may shift as AI platforms evolve.

---

### 5. Prepare for ChatGPT Ads (Low Signal)

**Tactic:** ChatGPT will launch ads combining Facebook's identity targeting with Google's intent signals.

**How:**
- Build topical authority now
- Use first-party data in ad platforms
- Create content that teaches AI who you help (be specific about audience)

**Applicability:** Any project planning paid acquisition.

**Priority:** LOW — Speculative. Worth monitoring but not actionable yet.

---

### 6. Prepare for Browser-Based AI (Low Signal)

**Tactic:** AI is moving into the browser layer (Chrome Gemini, etc.). Browsers will summarize and recommend before users reach websites.

**How:**
- Run Google AI Max campaigns to learn the system
- Add structured data / schema markup
- Treat site as data source, not just destination

**Applicability:** Any project with a website.

**Priority:** LOW — Future trend. Basic hygiene (schema markup) is worth doing now, but no urgent action.

---

### 7. Go Live Weekly (High Signal)

**Tactic:** Live video builds trust, spikes engagement, and creates repurposable content.

**How:**
- Go live once a week (doesn't need to be polished)
- Teach, answer questions, break down trends
- Let YouTube auto-generate vertical clips from livestreams
- Pull best 60-second moments for Shorts/Reels/TikTok

**Applicability:** Founder-led brands, personal brands, B2C, community-driven products.

**Priority:** HIGH for projects where founder visibility matters. MEDIUM otherwise.

---

### 8. Enable Multi-Language Audio (High Signal)

**Tactic:** YouTube can auto-dub your videos into dozens of languages using your voice.

**How:**
- Enable multi-language audio in YouTube settings
- Optimize website for global traffic (hreflang tags, language selectors)
- Localize CTAs and landing pages

**Applicability:** Any project with video content and potential international audience.

**Priority:** HIGH — Near-zero effort for YouTube. Website localization is more work but high ROI for right products.
